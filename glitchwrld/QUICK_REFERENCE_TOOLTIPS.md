## Usage

### How to View Tooltips
1. Navigate to solar system
2. Hover cursor over any celestial body
3. Read the symbolism
4. Move away to hide

### Tooltip Types
- **Planets**: Full detailed tooltips
- **Moons**: Compact tooltips

## Quick Tips

- **Hover, don't click** (unless you want to zoom in!)
- Tooltips appear above each body
- Each tooltip color-matches its planet
- Moons show simplified info
- All tooltips have mythology notes

## Fun Facts

- **Phobos & Deimos** orbit so close to Mars they'll eventually crash into it
- **Io** is the most volcanically active body in the solar system
- **Europa** may harbor more water than all Earth's oceans combined
- **Ganymede** is the largest moon in the solar system
- **Titan** is the only moon with a substantial atmosphere
- **Triton** orbits Neptune backwards (retrograde)
- **Venus** has emotional burning love interior design (unique to this project!)
